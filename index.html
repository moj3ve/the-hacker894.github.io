About Me

I am a NodeJS, Shell, and Frontend Developer. I have around 4 years of experience in programming. I primarily do programming in NodeJS working on the popular Discord Bot, CleverBot, as well as Shell/Bash scripting. I am also one of the maintainers for the website, Jailbreak Guide. You can find most of my projects on my GitHub Page.

I soon hope to being learning languages like Objective C, Swift, and Kotlin and endeavor into the world on making mobile apps.
